About

This site is a collection of open problems in mathematics, computer science, and related fields. Our goal is to:

  • Compile interesting unsolved problems in one place
  • Provide a space for discussion and collaboration
  • Make it easy to discover problems worth working on

Contributing

Anyone can submit a problem or join the discussion on existing ones. When submitting, please:

  • State the problem clearly and precisely
  • Include relevant background or references
  • Mention any known partial results

Contact

For questions or suggestions, reach out via the discussion threads.
